University Entrance Test

Chandigarh University conducts an entrance exam for admission to various courses. This test assesses the candidate's knowledge, critical thinking, and aptitude.

Entrance Test Syllabus

  • Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Trigonometry, and Statistics
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, and Biology fundamentals
  • English: Grammar, Vocabulary, and Comprehension
  • General Knowledge: Current events, History, and Geography

Preparation resources and previous years’ question papers are available in the library and online through the university portal.
